Cold Email Strategy Guide

Principles for Effective Cold Emails:

1. Keep It Short
- Maximum 150 words total
- Busy professionals scan emails in seconds
- Get to the point immediately

2. Compelling Subject Line
- 5-7 words maximum
- Be specific and relevant
- Examples: "DevOps role at [Company]", "Referred by [Name] - Software Engineer"

3. Personalization is Key
- Reference something specific about the company
- Mention a recent product launch, news, or initiative
- Show you've done your research

4. One Clear Ask
- Don't overwhelm with multiple requests
- Suggest a brief 10-15 minute call
- Make it easy to say yes

5. Highlight One Achievement
- Choose your most impressive and relevant accomplishment
- Include a specific number or metric
- Connect it directly to their needs

6. Professional but Personable
- Use a conversational tone
- Avoid overly formal language
- Show genuine interest and enthusiasm

7. Structure
- Subject line
- Brief introduction (1 sentence)
- Why you're reaching out (1-2 sentences)
- One standout achievement (1 sentence)
- Clear call to action (1 sentence)
- Professional signature

8. Timing and Follow-up
- Send during business hours
- Tuesday-Thursday typically best
- Follow up once after 5-7 days if no response
- Don't be pushy
